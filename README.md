# ⚡ FlowSpace

**Sustainable Productivity System**

A local-first, visual task manager designed for people who value consistency, focus, and clarity. No accounts. No servers. No distractions. Just you and your work.

## 🎯 The Philosophy

Most productivity tools are built for short bursts. They optimize for "getting things done today" but ignore the bigger picture: **long-term sustainability**.

FlowSpace takes a different approach:
- **Energy-aware scheduling** — Match tasks to your natural energy levels
- **Visual clarity** — See your progress at a glance, not buried in lists
- **One task at a time** — Flow Mode eliminates decision fatigue
- **Gentle tracking** — Understand your patterns without pressure
- **Weekly reflection** — Learn from your week, not just count tasks

This isn't about doing more. It's about doing what matters, consistently.

## ✨ Features

### 🌊 Flow Mode
Enter a distraction-free zone where only one task exists. Complete it, the next appears. Skip if needed. No judgment, no overwhelm. Just flow.

### 📊 Visual Activity Grid
See your productivity patterns over time. Green squares for productive days, empty for rest. Recognize your rhythms without pressure.

### 😊 Mood & Energy Tracking
Track how you feel before and after work. Understand which conditions help you thrive. Make data-driven decisions about when to tackle hard tasks.

### ⚠️ Avoid Tasks Detection
Tasks untouched for 3+ days get flagged. No shame, just awareness. Understand what you're avoiding and why.

### 📅 Weekly Review
Every weekend, get a comprehensive summary:
- Tasks completed
- Best and lowest days
- Mood trends
- Avoided tasks
- Reflection prompts for continuous improvement

### 🎨 8 Themes
From light minimal to dark midnight. Pick what feels right for your brain today. Your environment matters.

### 📋 Index System
All your tasks live in a categorized index. Schedule them on your timeline with drag-and-drop. No clutter, just clarity.

### 🔒 100% Local
Everything stays on your device. `localStorage` only. No cloud. No tracking. No accounts. Your data is yours forever.

### 📆 Weekly Schedule Template
Built-in structure for your week:
- **Sat/Sun** → Bug Bounty hunting
- **Mon** → Scripting & automation
- **Tue/Wed** → Bug Bounty practice
- **Thu** → Review & analysis
- **Fri** → Weekend rest

Customize it to fit your rhythm.
